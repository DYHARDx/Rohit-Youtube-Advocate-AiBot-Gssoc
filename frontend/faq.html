Frequently Asked Questions

What is YouTube Advisor? ▼
YouTube Advisor is an AI-based tool that helps creators handle legal documents, check content safety, and manage invoices.
Is it free to use? ▼
Basic features are free. Premium tools like detailed contract analysis or automated invoicing may require a subscription.
Does it store my data? ▼
Your uploaded files are processed securely and not stored permanently. Data privacy is a core principle.
Can it review YouTube policy changes? ▼
Yes. The system checks and updates YouTube’s policy summaries regularly to keep creators compliant.
How can I contact support? ▼
Use the contact form on the Contact page or email support@youtubeadvisor.ai.
